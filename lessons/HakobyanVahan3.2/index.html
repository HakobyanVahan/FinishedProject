What is CMS

CMS - << Content management system >> (engine) - a basis for the written PHP - programmers, with which you can manage the site (add content, change menu, items, etc) without knowing HTML and CSS.

However, in order to make a website using a CMS you will nedd the services of a programmera designer, and a layout designer. And investment.

What are CMS

There are various content managment systems: for online stores, for blogs, for forums, etc.

Examples of CMS

Examples of popular CMS: Joomla, WordPress,(for blogs), PhpBB (for forums).

There are paid and free CMSs.
